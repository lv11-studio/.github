# LV11 Studio

**LV11 Studio** is an indie game studio based in Brazil, focused on creating small, experimental and stylish games.

We are currently developing games through game jams, prototypes and short production cycles, exploring different mechanics, aesthetics and player experiences. Our goal is to keep building creative projects while growing into a studio capable of releasing original games and asset packs for other developers.

## 🎮 What we do

* Game jam projects
* Indie game prototypes
* Browser-playable games
* Experimental mechanics
* Pixel art, game feel and fast iteration
* Future asset packs for game developers

## 🕹️ Our games

You can play our current projects on itch.io:

* **Twin Titans** — a bullet hell journey where you control two pilots on stylish spaceships.
* **Bridgemancer** — a simple platformer where a wizard’s only magic is summoning bridges.
* **Alone in Whiteout** — a keyboard-only lighthouse rescue game built around isolation.
* **Kaltes Bureau** — our first game, made for Micro Jam 052.

👉 Play our games: [lv11-studio.itch.io](https://lv11-studio.itch.io)

## 🚀 Current focus

Right now, LV11 Studio is focused on learning, experimenting and building a solid creative pipeline through game jams and small scoped games.

Our next steps include:

* Improving our development workflow
* Publishing more jam games and prototypes
* Building reusable tools and internal systems
* Creating and selling asset packs
* Growing our portfolio as an indie game studio
