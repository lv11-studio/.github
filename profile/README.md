**LV11 Studio** is an indie game studio based in Brazil, for now focused on creating small, experimental and stylish games.

We are currently developing games through game jams, prototypes and short production cycles, exploring different mechanics, aesthetics and player experiences. Our goal is to keep building creative projects while growing into a studio capable of releasing original games and asset packs, full mechanics and tools for the game development community.

## 🎮 What we do

* Game jam projects
* Indie game prototypes
* Browser-playable games
* Godot experimental tools and reusable systems

## 🕹️ Our games

You can play our current projects on itch.io:

* **[Kaltes Bureau](https://lv11-studio.itch.io/kaltes-bureau)** — prowdly presenting our first game, winner on 1st place on the [Micro Jam 052](https://itch.io/jam/micro-jam-052).
* **[Twin Titans](https://lv11-studio.itch.io/twin-titans)** — also an award-winning game, 2nd place on the [Micro Jam 053](https://itch.io/jam/micro-jam-054).
* **[Bridgemancer](https://lv11-studio.itch.io/bridgemancer)** — a simple platformer where a wizard’s only magic is summoning bridge, 5th place by public vote on the [Code for a Cause x Microjam #4](https://itch.io/jam/cfac-x-micro-4).
* **[Alone in Whiteout](https://lv11-studio.itch.io/alone-in-whiteout)** — a keyboard-only lighthouse rescue game built around isolation, made for the [Micro Jam 054](https://itch.io/jam/micro-jam-054).

## 🚀 Current focus

Right now, LV11 Studio is focused on learning, experimenting and building a solid creative pipeline through game jams and small scoped games.

Our next steps include:

* Improving our development workflow
* Publishing more jam games and prototypes
* Building reusable tools and internal systems
* Creating and selling asset packs
* Growing our portfolio as an indie game studio
